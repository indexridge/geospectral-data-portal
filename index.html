Why pay if Sentinel data is free?

You are paying for convenience packaging: ready-to-load GeoTIFFs, source metadata, processing reports, README files, and disclaimers. The source imagery itself is open Sentinel data.

Are these complete global datasets?

No. They are regional sample AOI products and bundles, not full-country, continent, or global coverage.

Were they reviewed by a human?
